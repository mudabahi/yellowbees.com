Introduction

If you don't know what Web3 is, it's the next-level decentralized web that uses blockchain and other next-gen technologies to create a more secure and decentralized internet.

Our client is a pioneer in the Web3 space, and they asked us to help them craft their visual identity to showcase their innovation and dominance in this revolutionary field. We developed the most fire branding and built a website that met their goals and blew their minds.

Challenges

Crafting a brand and website for a Web3 company comes with some challenges. It's a complex and rapidly evolving technology that takes some know-how to understand its technical details and how it works. Communicating the benefits clearly and understandably to the average person can be challenging.

Last but not least, there are many companies in the Web3 space, and it can be challenging to create a brand and website that stands out from the competition and captures the attention of potential users.

Visual identity & website

The new visual identity and website we crafted for 49/Meta, a company in the cutting-edge WEB3 space, have been nothing short of transformative. Our extensive design expertise has forged a genuinely distinct brand that positions them leagues ahead of their competitors.

The combination of bespoke typography, captivating clay 3D illustrations, and dynamic gradients seamlessly integrates 49/Meta into the NFT and Web3 community, making them stand out as trailblazers in the industry.

Result

Our team's attention to detail and innovative design choices have resulted in a brand identity that resonates with the ethos of Web3 and captures the essence of 49/Meta's unique vision. The website, built for optimal user experience, provides a powerful platform for their message, solidifying their presence in this dynamic and evolving space.

Overall, our collaboration empowered 49/Meta with a visual identity and online presence that set them apart and propelled them to the forefront of the WEB3 revolution.
